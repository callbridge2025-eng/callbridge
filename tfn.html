How TFN provisioning works

1. Requirements & quote
You tell us the countries, anticipated monthly minutes, expected concurrency and routing preferences. We provide a customized quote and provisioning timeline.
2. Number provisioning
We provision toll-free numbers for the requested countries (subject to local carrier/regulatory processes) and confirm activation dates.
3. Routing & extensions
We configure unlimited extensions, IVR, agent queues and failover rules per your needs and integrate with your SIP / PSTN / CRM systems.
4. Go-live & support
We conduct tests, begin live routing, and provide monitoring and support. Enterprise SLAs can be part of your contract.

Common Questions

Can you provide toll-free numbers in every country?

We support provisioning in many countries; availability depends on local carriers and regulatory requirements. Contact sales with country list and we will confirm availability.

Are extensions really unlimited?

We configure unlimited extensions on request as part of the project scope — practical limits depend on call concurrency and backend SIP architecture. We'll size the solution to your needs.

How long does provisioning take?

Provisioning times vary by country and paperwork. Some countries activate in days; others may take weeks due to carrier/regulatory processes. Sales will provide estimated timelines per-country.
