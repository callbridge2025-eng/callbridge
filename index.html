Two products — different audiences

Softphone for individuals & SMBs — TFN for international contact centers

Call Bridge provides two dedicated offerings: a browser softphone (fast setup, Twilio-based PSTN calling, SMS/MMS, voicemail) for individuals and small teams — and TFN, a global toll-free-number & routing platform designed for offshore call centers and enterprise international operations.

Products & core features

Softphone — Ideal for individuals & SMBs
• Browser-based softphone (no client installs)
• Virtual number provisioning (TFN optional)
• Unlimited PSTN calling (plans) & in-browser DTMF
• Unlimited SMS & MMS with attachments
• Voicemail recordings, playback & downloads
• Simple account & user profile management
TFN — Designed for offshore & international call centers
• Global toll-free numbers for many countries
• Unlimited extensions & multi-agent routing
• Advanced call routing, IVR & failover rules
• High-volume inbound handling for contact centers
• Enterprise reporting, recording & integrations
Shared capabilities
• Secure login & per-user profiles
• Web APIs (backend hosted on Render)
• SMS threading, file attachments & delivery updates
• Responsive UI for desktop & mobile browsers
Compliance & reliability
• Call recording and storage options
• Geo-aware routing & regional compliance support
• SLA options for enterprise TFN customers
Integrations
• Twilio-powered voice & messaging (softphone)
• Easy webhook hooks for CRM / ticketing systems
• Custom integrations available for TFN customers
Getting started
• Softphone: signup → assign virtual number → call & message
• TFN: contact sales for provisioning global TFNs & routing
