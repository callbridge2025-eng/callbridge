Make calls, manage numbers, and handle messages — all from one place.

Call Bridge is a lightweight web softphone with virtual telephone numbers (TFN), SMS threading, and voicemail — built for teams and small businesses. No downloads, works in-browser.

What you get

Browser softphone
Make/receive calls using Twilio, DTMF, mute, hold & speaker controls.
Virtual Numbers (TFN)
Assign and manage telephone numbers for your team.
SMS & MMS
Threaded conversations, attachments, and delivery statuses.
Voicemail
Recordings stored and played directly from the browser.
Admin & Billing
Profile, expiration, and user management APIs.
Mobile-ready
Works in modern mobile browsers and WebView (for Play Store WebView).
